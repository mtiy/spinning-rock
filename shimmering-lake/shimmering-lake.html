Shimmering Lake

You hear water lapping at your feet, and the sound of crickets chirping all around.
You are on the edge of a lake. Across you can see dark structures, and in the middle you see a small island. Moonlight reflects off the surface.
You reach down and feel smooth, flat stones in the water.
Something is rotten here. An awful smell, permeating through everything.
The water tastes sour and acidic.
