Silent Desert

There is nothing, not the wind nor the sound of animals. Only silence.
The dunes of the desert are black against the starry sky. Stretching for infinity.
You take a handful of sand and let it slip through your fingers. It is soft and cool to the touch.
There is no smell, nor wind to carry it if it does exist.
The sand is awful - why did you do this? It coats your mouth and throat and…wait. Something sweet bursts. You look more carefully. There are berries underneath the sand!
