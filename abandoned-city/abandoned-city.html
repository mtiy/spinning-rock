Abandoned City

You hear the groaning and creaking of metal.
Around you are dark, impossibly tall towers. You see streets cracked down the middle, piles of paper and plastic covering the ground. The windows are shattered and boarded up. But in the distance, you see a dim light emanating from a storefront.
The piles of trash are covered in a layer of white ash that coats your fingers.
The dust and ash clog your nose, making it impossible to smell anything.
You dig through the trash and find a sealed, cream-filled pastry. It is delicious.
