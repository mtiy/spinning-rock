Abandoned City

You hear the groaning and creaking of metal.
Around you are dark, impossibly tall towers. You see streets cracked down the middle, piles of paper and plastic covering the ground. The windows are shattered and boarded up.
The piles of trash are covered in a layer of white ash that coats your fingers.
The dust and ash clog your nose, making it impossible to smell anything.
You find a twinkie on the ground. It is delicious. You see a few more a bit farther down the road.
