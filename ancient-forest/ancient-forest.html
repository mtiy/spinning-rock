Ancient Forest

You hear the wind shaking the leaves of trees overhead. In the undergrowth you hear rustling.
You are in a clearing. All around you are the trunks of tall, dark trees. Above bits of dark blue sky peek through the canopy.
The tree bark is soft and squishy.
You smell wet bark and dirt, and the wind carries with it a faint scent of smoke.
The dirt tastes strange - wait, why did you just eat dirt?
